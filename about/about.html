What are Bullet Journals?

In the past few years, bullet journals have been popping up all over social media, from Pinterest photos to Buzzfeed articles, so what actually are they?

Bullet journals are a new take on a diary. In todays world there are lots of stresses on daily life, causing us to forget to look after ourselves properly. Enter the bullet journal! Bullet journals are customisable diaries which usually include things like habit trackers, lists, calendars and many more exciting things to help you keep track of day to day life!

So, what makes the bullet journal stand out from a diary?

Well, bullet journals are made by you! You can be as artistic as you like with it, design the pages in whatever order you like and however you like, theres no rules!
