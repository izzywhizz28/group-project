Collections for your Bullet Journal

What are Lists & Trackers?

Habit trackers are a useful tool in Bullet Journals. They can help you see how you are progressing with medication, or your mood, or weight loss.

Some ideas of trackers are given below

  • Medication tracker
  • Mood tracker
  • Weight loss/gain tracker
  • 5 a day tracker
  • Spendings/savings tracker

Lists and Collections

Lists are commonly used to remember things which you want to do in the future, or for tasks you need to complete.

Examples of lists are:

  • Films to watch
  • Books to read
  • Places to travel
  • Day trips to go on
  • Bucket list
  • Chores
  • Master shopping list
  • Games to play
  • Birthday/christmas Gifts list

There are lots of common ideas around for lists, such as Netflix collections to watch, but you can make them personal too such as the chores list.
