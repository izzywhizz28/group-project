How to create your very own Bullet Journal

What is a Bullet Journal?

Fundamentally, a bullet journal is a customisable diary. You make it as stylish and colourful and include whatever you like. Find out more about bullet journals here!

What do I need to make a Bullet Journal?

Not much! To make your own Bullet Journal all you need is:

  • An unused notebook (blank, dotted or squared)
  • A pen
  • A ruler
  • Some crayons (felts work too!)
  • You!
Of course, there are more things you can use than that, which you can find more on here, but the essential items are listed above.
