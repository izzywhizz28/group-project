You've no doubt heard the words Bullet Journal at some point in the past year or so, or at least seen images of one somewhere. Carry on reading and you'll find out everything you need to know, from the history of Bullet Journals to tricks on making your journal look the best it can be!

What do I need to make a Bullet Journal?

Not much! To make your own Bullet Journal all you need is:

  • A noteboook
  • A pen
  • A ruler
  • Some crayons or felts
  • A bit of creativity
Of course, there are more things you can use than that, which you can find more on here, but these are the core items.
